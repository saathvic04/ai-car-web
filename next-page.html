The Road to Self-Driving Cars

Functions

Picture a world where your car has a better sense of direction than you do and never argues with you about the best route. The road to self-driving cars is like giving your car a brain transplant and hoping it doesn't develop a rebellious streak. These intelligent machines, armed with more sensors than a spy movie gadget, are learning to navigate our chaotic streets. They stop for pedestrians, avoid rogue shopping carts, and never complain about your choice of music. As we get closer to fully autonomous vehicles, we’re preparing for a future where "designated driver" means your car has its own driver's license, and traffic jams are just stories to tell your grandkids. Just imagine, a car that can drop you off at the office and then go find its own parking spot – it's like having a personal chauffeur, minus the small talk!
